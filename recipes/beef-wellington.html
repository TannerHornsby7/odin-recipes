Beef Wellington Recipe

Description

A traditional Beef Wellington consists of a beef tenderloin wrapped in layers of pâté, duxelles (a finely chopped mushroom mixture), parma ham, and puff pastry, then baked. Rumor has it that Beef Wellington got its name from Arthur Wellesley, the 1st Duke of, who counted the dish among his favorite recipes.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Lay out sheets
  2. Put on Butter
  3. Put pasta sauce and meat on
  4. Cook for 45 minutes at 325
  5. Enjoy!
